# 📌 Problem Statement

## Smart Government Scheme Finder for India (SchemeSaathi)

---

## 🔹 1. Background

India offers **3000+ central and state government schemes** aimed at providing support in areas such as:

* Financial assistance
* Healthcare
* Education
* Housing
* Employment and livelihood

Despite this, a significant portion of eligible citizens **do not benefit from these schemes**.

---

## ⚠️ 2. Core Problem

Millions of eligible beneficiaries — especially from:

* Rural areas
* Low-income households
* Marginalized communities

**fail to discover or apply for schemes they qualify for.**

---

## ❗ 3. Why This Problem Exists

The gap between schemes and citizens is caused by:

* **Fragmented Information**
  Schemes are distributed across multiple government portals, PDFs, and websites

* **Complex Eligibility Criteria**
  Written in bureaucratic and hard-to-understand language

* **Lack of Guidance**
  Citizens are unaware of:

  * Required documents
  * Application procedures
  * Where to apply

* **No Personalization**
  Existing platforms do not provide **profile-based recommendations**

---

## 💡 4. Proposed Solution — SchemeSaathi

SchemeSaathi is an **AI-powered agent** that helps users discover and apply for relevant government schemes.

### 🔧 How It Works:

1. **User Profiling**
   Collects user details such as:

   * Age
   * Gender
   * State
   * Income
   * Occupation
   * Category
   * Special conditions

2. **Real-Time Search**
   Uses Tavily to fetch updated schemes from trusted sources

3. **Eligibility Matching**
   Filters schemes based on the user’s profile

4. **Guided Output**
   Provides:

   * Eligibility explanation
   * Benefits
   * Required documents
   * Step-by-step application process
   * Official links

5. **Self-Evaluation (LLM-as-Judge)**
   Ensures output quality, relevance, and clarity

---

## 🎯 5. Target Users

* Farmers
* Students
* Daily wage workers
* Women
* Differently-abled individuals
* BPL families
* First-time applicants with low digital awareness

---

## 🌍 6. Impact

* Bridges the **last-mile gap** between citizens and welfare schemes
* Improves awareness and accessibility
* Reduces dependency on middlemen
* Enables informed decision-making
* Promotes inclusive access to government benefits

---

## 🚀 7. Why Agentic AI?

A traditional system cannot:

* Handle incomplete or ambiguous user input
* Adapt questioning dynamically
* Continuously fetch updated scheme data

An **agent-based AI system** can:

* Ask intelligent follow-up questions
* Use tools (Tavily) for real-time search
* Perform reasoning-based eligibility matching
* Generate personalized guidance

---

## ✅ 8. Outcome

A scalable AI system that delivers:

* Accurate scheme recommendations
* Personalized eligibility insights
* Clear application guidance

Making government benefits **accessible, understandable, and actionable** for every citizen.
